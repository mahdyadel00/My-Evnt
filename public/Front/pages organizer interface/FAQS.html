Frequently Asked Questions

If you encounter any difficulties while using our ticket selling platform, you can always count on our assistance. Event Team is here to answer all of your questions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you charge commissions on tickets sold online?

The payment system we use to receive and process payments, Stripe, imposes a fee of 3.5% + £0.25 on ticket buyers based on the total purchase price. If you choose an alternative payment method, the commission will be 3.5% + £0.25 + our service commission of 8%. With an alternative payment method, organizers can decide whether they or the buyer will cover the commission.

Can I sell tickets offline?

Sure. During the event creation process, you can choose the "Ticket Office" option as the payment method. After your event is approved, you can generate and download tickets from your personal account’s "My Events" section for subsequent sale.

What is moderation, and how long does it takes?

Moderation is the process of approval of an event by the administration of the ME-Ticket site. At this stage, your event is verified to ensure compliance with the platform’s terms and conditions. Additionally, the event is checked for possible errors. For example, incorrect name of the event location, error in the start and end times of the event, etc.If your event is approved for moderation, it will immediately become available on the site for ticket purchases. If your event is rejected, you will receive an email with a comment from the moderator, indicating the reasons for rejection along with recommendations for correction. You can edit the event and submit it for re-moderation. Moderation usually takes less than 12 hours.

Who can register as an organizer on your site?

Any person who organizes events, as well as one-time or regular activities.
