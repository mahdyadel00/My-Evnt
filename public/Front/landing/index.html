About The Event

Clash of Champions: Al Ahly vs Al Ain - 2024 Intercontinental Cup Showdown

On October 29, 2024, Egyptian giants Al Ahly face off against UAE's Al Ain in the second round of the Intercontinental Cup at Cairo International Stadium. Both teams are battling for a spot in the third stage, where they could meet the champions of the 2024 Copa Libertadores or the 2024 CONCACAF winner. Al Ahly, with their strong lineup, aim to secure a crucial victory, while Al Ain seeks a positive result to stay in the race. Fans can catch the game live on various free channels, including MBC MASR 2 and ON TIME SPORTSs
